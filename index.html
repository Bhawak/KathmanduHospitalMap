Welcome to Hamro Hospital!





Here you can find all the hospital in Kathmandu Valley with the facilities and services they provide. The map is easy to navigate. By clicking on the Hospital the information about the hospitals are displayed. The information regarding the services like ultrasound, ECG, CT scan, ECO, the blood test specification, and etc are enlisted. The facilities like number of bed, number of doctors and the physicians are also alongside the facilities. This website is created so that the patient will get the information about the hospital easily in the virtual enviornment.
